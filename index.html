Precious Lord

This is by far the most popular song written by the "Father of Gospel Music", Dr. Thomas Dorsey. It was written following the death of his wife and child. Thomas Dorsey was born in Georgia and was the pianist for many jazz singers including Ma Rainey.

Walk Around Heaven

Walking around heaven was and is the dream of many African American Christians. With life as it was then and is now, many dreamed of a day when you could just "Walk Around Heaven."

High Way to Heaven

"High Way to Heaven" is a song that is often misunderstood. Some have renamed it as "It's a 'highway' to heaven" meaning something with two, four or six lanes. But the real meaning is more associated with, it's a 'high' way. Meaning that we have to live above sin, debauchery and evilness. We have to take the 'high' way not the 'low'way.
